What is this?

IFFMC Data is an open source database of routes, Standard Instrument Departures (SIDs), and Standard Terminal Arrivals (STARs) that can be loaded into IFFMC easily via the RTE program.

What are some use cases?

IFFMC Data opens up a variety of ways to explore the power of IFFMC and Infinite Flight. We invision that IFFMC Data will be able to provide some pre-made routes for VA's or otherwise. Additionally, in the short term, IFFMC Data will be the host of SIDs and STARs for airports.
